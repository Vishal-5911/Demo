Experiences of Virat Kohli

Early life

Kohli's talent was evident from a young age, and he was active, eager, and dedicated to the sport. He was promoted to bat up the order in an under-15 game when he was 12 years old, scoring a century to lead his team to victory.

Domestic debut

Kohli made his domestic debut for Delhi in a one-day match against Services in 2006. He scored only 10 runs in his first-class debut against Tamil Nadu later that year.

International debut

Kohli made his international debut in an ODI in 2008, opening the batting and scoring 12 in a defeat of Sri Lanka.

ICC Under-19 World Cup

Kohli captained the Under-19 Indian cricket team to victory at the ICC Under-19 World Cup in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, in 2008.

Personal losses

Kohli's early life was marked by personal losses, including the death of his father from a cerebral attack in 2006.

Marriage

Kohli married Bollywood actress Anushka Sharma on December 11, 2017 in Florence, Italy. They had their first child, a daughter named Vamika, on January 11, 2021.

Adaptability

Kohli's ability to adapt to changes in the world of cricket, such as changing strategies, varying pitches, and adapting opponents, is inspiring.
