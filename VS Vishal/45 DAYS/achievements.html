Achievments of Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli, one of the greatest cricketers of all time, has achieved numerous milestones in his illustrious career. Here are some of his most notable achievements: International Cricket Runs and Centuries: Over 25,000 international runs across all formats (Tests, ODIs, T20Is). 75+ international centuries (as of 2025), ranking him second only to Sachin Tendulkar in terms of centuries. Fastest Records: Fastest to 8,000, 9,000, 10,000, 11,000, and 12,000 ODI runs. First player to score three consecutive centuries in ODIs twice. Fastest to score 10,000 ODI runs in just 205 innings. Consistency Across Formats: First player to average over 50 in Tests, ODIs, and T20Is simultaneously. As a Captain Test Cricket: India's most successful Test captain with 40 wins. Led India to the No. 1 ICC Test ranking for several years. Captained India in the inaugural ICC World Test Championship Final (2021). ODI and T20 Success: Led India to victory in the 2018 Asia Cup and several bilateral series. Captained India to its first-ever Test series win in Australia (2018-19). Aggressive Leadership: Known for instilling a fitness culture and aggressive mindset in the Indian team. Awards and Honors ICC Awards: ICC Cricketer of the Year (2017, 2018). ICC ODI Player of the Year (2012, 2017, 2018). ICC Test Player of the Year (2018). Named in the ICC ODI and Test Teams of the Decade (2011-2020). National Honors: Arjuna Award (2013).
